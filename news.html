UMTH MANAGEMENT APPOINTS SHEHU OF BORNO AS THE ROYAL FATHER OF THE DAY FOR THE FORTHCOMING UMTH 40TH ANNIVERSARY

The Management of the University of Maiduguri Teaching Hospital has appointed Shehu of Borno, Alhaji (Dr) Muhammad Garbai Al-amin El-kanemi as the Royal father of the day for the Hospital's forthcoming 40th Anniversary celebration commencing on 13th July, 2023. The Chief Medical Director UMTH, Professor Ahmed Ahidjo presented the letter of appointment today when he led the Top Management Committee of the Hospital to the Shehu's Palace for Eid-El-Kabeer greeting. Prof. Ahidjo while presenting the letter, said that the Management consider it necessary to appoint Shehu of Borno as the Royal Father for the celebration of 40 years of quality healthcare delivery to good people of Borno because of his continued support and commitment to the development of the Hospital. Recalled that, UMTH will be celebrating its 40 years of excellence in healthcare service delivery to people of North-East Nigeria and Neighboring Countries of Niger, Cameroun and Chad Republics commencing from 12th July to 22nd July, 2023. The Shehu of Borno, Alhaji (Dr) Muhammad Garbai Al-amin El-kanemi while appreciating the Chief Medical Director and his team for finding him worthy and the warmth visit, extolled the Chief Medical Director for his commitment in the development of the Hospital and the delivery of quality healthcare services in Borno State and Neighboring States. He also said that healthcare sector needs committed people like Prof. Ahidjo who works day and night for serving humanity.

About

The University of Maiduguri Teaching Hospital (UMTH) was officially commissioned on the 23rd of July, 1983 by former President Aliyu Shehu Shagari, GCFR. The hospital now has total bed capacity of 1,200 with 17 clinical and 14 non clinical Departments; serving a population of over 30 million in the North East Geopolitical Zone.
